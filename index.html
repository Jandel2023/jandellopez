About

Built for craft, tuned for speed.

Jandel Lopez is a product designer and front-end developer who likes things that feel fast — clean interfaces, considered motion, and code that doesn't get in the way of the idea.

The work spans early-stage product design, design systems, and the front-end builds that bring them to life. A few years split between agency work and independent projects landed on a simple rule: good craft is mostly discipline, not decoration.

Currently — designing and building for early-stage product teams.
